What if you cannot make it?

Do not panic! We have solutions for you!


Send a representative

You can send a friend or a family member to collect your kit on your behalf. Please be mindful of the requirements. If your representative does not have the required documents, they will not be able to collect your kit.

What to bring:
  1. Representative's Identification (NRIC or FIN or passport)
  2. Printed copy of signed Authorisation Form
  3. E-copy of Participants Identification (NRIC or FIN or passport)
  4. E-copy of Participants entry confirmation email with the QR code
  5. A printed copy of Participants completed PAR-Q Form



Have it delivered

The courier service is $22 and $10 for every extra person to the same address. Email us to request this service or add it on when you register for the race. Event participants must make sure to book before the deadline, 10 FEBRUARY 2019. Race Kit Deliveries will be on 13 FEBRUARY 2019.

You may request for a delivery here!


Overseas Participants

If you are an overseas participant, you can collect your race kit on race day from 7am at Information booth.
