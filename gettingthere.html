A Guide For Your Various Race Destinations

Getting There

SUNDAY, 17 FEB 2019 PALAWAN BEACH, SENTOSA

AQUATHLON

Race Venue: Palawan Beach, Sentosa 200m walk from the Beach Station

How to get there

  • By Car: Park at the Beach Station Car Park
  • By Taxi: Alight at the Beach Station taxi stop
  • By Bus: Buses no. 65, 80, 93, 188, 855, 10, 30, 97, 100, 131, 143, 145, 166 stop at Harbour Front Bus Interchange/Telok Blangah Road
  • By Shuttle Bus: FREE Sentosa-MetaSprint Series shuttle bus (6:45am to 9:45am) between Beach Station and HarbourFront Bus Interchange (Seah Im Road)
  • By MRT: The North East Line or Circle Line and alight at the HarbourFront Station. From there go to the 3rd level of VivoCity shopping mall and transfer onto the Sentosa Express
  • By Sentosa Express:
    • Take the Sentosa Express from Vivocity (Level 3, Lobby L) to the Beach Station at Sentosa
    • This service starts 7am and takes 5-10min

SUNDAY, 10 MAR 2019 F1 VILLAGE

DUATHLON

Race Venue: F1 Village – 20 Republic Avenue, Singapore 038970

How to get there

  • Cycle straight to the race: Park at the Beach Station Car Park
  • By Car: Park at One of the many car parks in the area
    • The Singapore Flyer
    • Marina Square
    • The Ritz Carlton Millenia Singapore
    • Millenia Walk
    • Mandarin Oriental Singapore
  • By Taxi: Alight at the F1 Pit Building. Walk 450m north of the F1 Pit Building (15-min walk)
  • By Train: The nearest MRT station is Nicoll Highway Station, Circle Line stop CC5. Note the most trains are running around 0600 to 0615am only.
    Note: Bikes are not allowed in the train. Take the train only if you are using a folding bike or is renting one at the venue.
  • By Bus: Buses no. NR2, NR5, NR7, NR8, 1N, 2N, 3N, 4N, 5N, 6N, 56, 75, 77, 97, 171, 195, 960 stop at Raffles Boulevard Bus Terminal. Check the bus schedule before considering taking the bus.

REMINDER TO BE ON TIME: Arrive at the venue 45 mins before the race starts!

SUNDAY, 28 APR 2019 EAST COAST PARK

TRIATHALON

Race Venue: East Coast Park, Near the Food Village

How to get there

  • By Car: Park at one of the many car parks in the area:
    • Carpark D1 – D5
    • Carpark E1 – E3
    • Marina Parade HDB estate carparks Alight at East Coast Park Lagoon Food Centre (Carpark E2).
    • By Train: The nearest MRT station is BEDOK STATION, East West Line stop EW5. Note the most trains are running around 0600 to 0610am only. Note: Bikes are not allowed in the train. Take the train only if you are using a folding bike or are renting one at the venue.
